How to convert Word to PDF

  1. Drag and drop your Word (.docx) file into the box above or click to select a file
  2. Click the "Convert to PDF" button
  3. Wait for the conversion to complete (usually just a few seconds)
  4. Download your new PDF file
Note: Complex formatting may not convert perfectly as this is a browser-based solution.

How to convert PDF to Word

  1. Drag and drop your PDF file into the box above or click to select a file
  2. Click the "Convert to Word" button
  3. Wait for the conversion to complete
  4. Download your new Word document
Note: This conversion extracts text and basic formatting. Complex layouts may not be preserved perfectly.

How to convert PowerPoint to PDF

  1. Drag and drop your PowerPoint (.ppt or .pptx) file into the box above
