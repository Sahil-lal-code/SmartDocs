How to compress images

  1. Drag and drop your image (JPG, PNG, WebP) or click to select a file
  2. Adjust compression settings as needed
  3. Click the "Compress Image" button
  4. Compare the original and compressed versions
  5. Download your compressed image
Tip: For best results, try different quality levels to find the right balance between size and quality.
